The aiport in Kauai, Lihue Airport, is located on the south east part of the island.

You can go to Google Flights to book a flight right now.

If you want to visit multiple islands or even just make a day trip, flights between islands are fairly cheap. Roundtrip, it will likely be less then 100$.
